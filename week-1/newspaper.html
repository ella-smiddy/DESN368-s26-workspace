Cat Stuck in a Tree, Saved by Firemen

How It Happened

It was 8 AM, April 05, 2026. A poor cat, trying to escape a neighborhood dog, finds shelter up a tall tree.
The dog eventually gets bored and leaves the cat alone around 9 AM. Unfortunately, the cat is left stuck
way up high and cannot find a safe way down. The cat realized quickly that she had gotten herself in
trouble. Firefighters were called to the scene around 9:15 AM.

The Struggle

The cat was scared, left in unkown territory. It meowed and hissed at the firemen, not sure who to trust.
The firemen were worried she would knock herself off from all the comotion and movement. Suddenly,
one of the firemen got an idea! He has left over chicken from yesterday's lunch in the truck. He uses this
to lure the cat towards them, closer to safety. The cat seemed hungry and ready for an easy breakfast.
The firefighters were very still until, eventually, the cat takes the bait, and is safely grabbed and
brought down to the ground again.p>
